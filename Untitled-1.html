Course Introduction

The course is about understanding how to design efficient algorithms and analyze their performance in terms of time and space complexity. It covers key concepts like problem-solving techniques and methods to measure and optimize algorithms. The focus is on improving problem-solving skills, understanding trade-offs in computational performance, and learning how to select the right approach for different types of problems.
